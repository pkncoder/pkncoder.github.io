DnD Quick Lookup

Dnd has many spells, monsters, class information, and more. Sometimes it can be a chalange as big as a dungeon to remember it all. Thankfully we have the DnD Quick Lookup. Whenever you, being the DM, need to find the spesifics for a spell from offical rules, just type it in. With a quick choice from 1 to 4, 1: spells, 2: monsters, 3: class info, and 4: anything a it more spisific, you can find almost anything! Quick and easy is best!
